📋 How to Export CSVs

Chase:

  1. Log in to Chase.com
  2. Go to Plan & Track
  3. Click Budget (scroll down on the right under "More financial tools")
  4. Click Download transactions
  5. Choose your date range
  6. Download and save the file

Discover:

  1. Log in to Discover.com
  2. Click Activity from the navbar at the top
  3. Click Search Transactions
  4. Select your date range and click Search
  5. Click Download
  6. Choose CSV format and save the file
💡 Tip: Transactions are automatically synced to your Google Sheet if configured! Duplicate transactions are automatically skipped.

🏷️ About Category Options

Use CSV Categories: Import categories exactly as your bank labeled them. Great if your bank's categories are already accurate.

Use Auto-Tagging Rules: Apply your custom rules with matching keywords. Set these up in the dashboard under "Category Rules". Transactions that don't match any rule will remain uncategorized.

Note: Recurrence defaults to "One-time" unless an auto-tag rule changes it.
