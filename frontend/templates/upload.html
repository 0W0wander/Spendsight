📋 How to Export CSVs

Chase:

  1. Log in to Chase.com
  2. Select your account
  3. Go to "Activity" or "Transactions"
  4. Click "Download" and select "CSV"
  5. Choose your date range
  6. Download and save the file

Discover:

  1. Log in to Discover.com
  2. Go to "Recent Activity"
  3. Click "Download" icon
  4. Select "CSV" format
  5. Choose your date range
  6. Download and save the file
💡 Tip: Transactions are automatically synced to your Google Sheet if configured!
